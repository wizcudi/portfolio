About Me

Hi! I'm Tony, Concert Promoter turned Web Developer. Mainly known for my events in Orlando, booking acts such as Post Malone, Social Club Misfits and a few others. In the wake of the 2020 pandemic I decided to learn Web Development.

My goal is to offer these new found skills to small business owners looking to set up their online presence and also to apply these skills into my own personal ventures.

In my free time I like to improve on my coding skills by challenge myself with new coding projects. I also like to discover new music.
